Profesora: Claudia Pellegrini

Completa los ejercicios según las consignas. Al finalizar, exporta tus respuestas en PDF.

Before watching

1. Work in small groups. Discuss the questions.

  1. What’s your hometown like?
  2. Look at the pictures. Where is this? What do you know about this city?

While watching

2. Watch the video. Tick (✓) the places that are mentioned.

3. Watch A New York life. Answer the questions.

  1. Why does Nick like living in New York?
  2. What does he like most about living in New York?
  3. What reputation do New Yorkers have? What does Nick think about them?
  4. What does Nick like about his neighbourhood?
  5. What’s his apartment like?
  6. What does Nick like doing in his free time?

4. Watch A family story. Underline the correct options.

  1. Like many other European immigrants, Nick’s father’s family came to New York to / .
  2. Nick’s grandparents met when / with an everyday task.
  3. Nick’s parents met in / .
  4. Nick looks just like his / , according to everybody else.
  5. Nick’s family often go to the opera when they visit / / .

5. Watch If you can make it here ... Are the sentences true (✓) or false (✗)? Correct the false sentences.

  1. His mother is artistic, but his father is more of a businessman.

  2. Nick thinks he received positive qualities from his mum and his dad.

  3. Nick has started making movies recently.

  4. Nick is always doing one project at a time.

  5. Most of the footage that Nick shoots goes into the final video.

  6. Nick hopes that his films will make the world a better place.

After watching

6. Work with a partner. Discuss the questions.

  • Why do you think people are so interested to learn about their family history?
  • Do you want to know more about your own family history? Why/Why not?
